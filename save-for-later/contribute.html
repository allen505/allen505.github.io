Welcome to the contribute page of Save for Later

Thank you for choosing Save for Later, a simple web extension to simplify managing and storing tabs. This page lists various ways in which you could help contribute to your favourite extension. If you've enjoyed using Save for Later as much as I've enjoyed creating it, you can contribute to it's devlopment in any of the following ways.

Monetary support

Support this project by providing monetary support. This helps in further devlopment of the product and adding features to make the extension even more functional helping more people in the community.

You can also spread the word by sharing this extension with your friends, family and co-workers. Know someone who could really use this extension? You know the drill.
